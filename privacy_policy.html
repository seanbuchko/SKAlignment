Privacy Policy for SKAlignmentr

Last updated: 2025-07-08

1. Information We Collect

The App uses your device’s GPS location to determine the nearest highway and related geospatial information. This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or transmitted to any server.

2. Use of Data

The App uses official geospatial data licensed under the Government of Saskatchewan Standard Unrestricted Use Data Licence (Version 2.0). The data is used solely to provide highway location, stationing, and offset information.

3. Data Sharing

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. No data is transmitted to third parties.

4. Attribution

“Contains information licensed under the Government of Saskatchewan Standard Unrestricted Use Data Licence (Version 2.0).”

5. Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to the use of your location data as described in this policy.
